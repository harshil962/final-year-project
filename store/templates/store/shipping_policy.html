Shipping Policy

We ensure your orders reach you quickly, safely, and reliably across India.

At Medicart, we aim to deliver your medicines and healthcare products in the fastest and safest way possible. We currently deliver across India through our trusted logistics partners.

Delivery Time

Orders are usually processed within 24 hours and delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping Charges

- Free shipping for orders above ₹500.
- A nominal charge of ₹50 applies for smaller orders.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an email and SMS with tracking details. You can also track your order in the “My Orders” section on our website.

Delays and Exceptions

While we strive to meet delivery timelines, delays may occur due to weather, public holidays, or unforeseen logistical issues. In such cases, we will notify you promptly.

Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, please contact our support team at support@Medicart.com.
